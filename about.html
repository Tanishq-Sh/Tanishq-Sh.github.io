About Me

A little bit about my background and journey.

Hello! I am a passionate engineer and lifelong learner. After an incredible 4.5 years at Apple, where I contributed to large scale data engineering, machine learning systems and customer facing intelligence platforms used across AppleCare, I decided to dive deeper into the field that fascinates me most, which is Artificial Intelligence.

During my time at Apple, I architected Snowflake based data platforms, built LLM powered applications for advisor support, engineered GPU accelerated ML pipelines, deployed NLP services, automated CI/CD workflows and designed scalable data models and orchestration pipelines that supported billions of user interactions. These experiences strengthened my curiosity about how intelligent systems can be built responsibly and at scale.

I am currently a full time student at Imperial College London, working towards my MSc in Artificial Intelligence. I am exploring topics like Reinforcement Learning, Deep Learning and the mathematical foundations of ML. My goal is to use this knowledge to build intelligent, impactful and responsible technology that solves complex problems and empowers people in meaningful ways.
